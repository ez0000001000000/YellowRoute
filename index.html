Frequently Asked Questions

We proudly serve all of South Florida, including Miami Beach, Hollywood, Surfside, Aventura, Highland Lakes, and North Miami Beach.

Our transportation services operate Monday through Friday, with morning pickups starting at 6:30 AM and afternoon drop-offs completed by 6:00 PM.

You can sign up by filling out the contact form on our website or giving us a call at (786) 832-0003. We'll be happy to discuss your transportation needs.

All our drivers are fully licensed and undergo thorough background checks. Our vehicles are regularly maintained and equipped with seat belts and first aid kits.

We require at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations. Please contact us as soon as possible if you need to cancel or reschedule a ride.
